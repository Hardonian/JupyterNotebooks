# Switch Moments - Agent Factory

**For:** Jobs-to-Be-Done, GTM Strategy, Messaging  
**Last Updated:** 2024-01-XX

---

## Overview

This document identifies the moments when customers switch from alternatives to Agent Factory. Understanding switch moments helps us target the right customers at the right time with the right messaging.

---

## Switch Moment Definition

**Switch Moment:** The specific moment or situation that causes a customer to switch from their current alternative to Agent Factory.

**Key Characteristics:**
- **Trigger:** What causes the switch?
- **Emotion:** How does the customer feel?
- **Action:** What does the customer do?
- **Timing:** When does this happen?

---

## Primary Switch Moments

### Switch Moment 1: Prototype Works, But Can't Deploy

**Trigger:**
- Prototype works beautifully in notebook/local environment
- Try to deploy to production
- Hit infrastructure wall (auth, billing, deployment, etc.)
- Realize deployment is blocked

**Emotion:**
- Frustrated (prototype works, but can't use it)
- Blocked (can't move forward)
- Overwhelmed (infrastructure seems complex)
- Urgent (need to ship)

**Action:**
- Search for solutions ("how to deploy AI agent", "AI agent deployment")
- Try alternatives (LangChain, cloud platforms)
- Discover Agent Factory
- Sign up for free tier

**Timing:**
- After prototype is working
- Before giving up on deployment
- When time becomes critical

**Messaging:**
- "Your prototype works. Now deploy it to production in minutes."
- "Stop wrestling with infrastructure. Start shipping."
- "From notebook to production in one command."

**Target Audience:**
- Solo founders with working prototypes
- Researchers with notebook prototypes
- Developers who've hit deployment wall

**Frequency:** Very common (90%+ of users experience this)

---

### Switch Moment 2: Infrastructure Costs Too High

**Trigger:**
- Realize infrastructure costs (time, money) are too high
- Calculate cost of hiring engineers ($100K+/year)
- Calculate time cost (months of work)
- Realize it's not worth it

**Emotion:**
- Cost-conscious (can't afford engineers)
- Overwhelmed (costs are too high)
- Resource-constrained (limited budget)
- Anxious (worried about costs)

**Action:**
- Search for alternatives ("affordable AI agent platform", "AI agent infrastructure cost")
- Compare costs (Agent Factory vs. hiring)
- Realize Agent Factory is much cheaper
- Sign up for free tier

**Timing:**
- After calculating infrastructure costs
- Before committing to expensive solution
- When budget becomes constraint

**Messaging:**
- "Get infrastructure expertise for $199/month, not $150K/year."
- "Affordable infrastructure for solo founders."
- "10x cheaper than hiring engineers."

**Target Audience:**
- Solo founders with limited budget
- Startups with resource constraints
- Cost-conscious developers

**Frequency:** Common (50%+ of users experience this)

---

### Switch Moment 3: Time Becomes Critical

**Trigger:**
- Need to ship fast
- Competitors moving faster
- Deadline approaching
- Can't wait months for infrastructure

**Emotion:**
- Urgent (need to move fast)
- Pressured (deadline approaching)
- Competitive (competitors moving faster)
- Stressed (time running out)

**Action:**
- Search for fast solutions ("fast AI agent deployment", "quick AI agent platform")
- Look for time-saving solutions
- Discover Agent Factory (deploy in days, not months)
- Sign up immediately

**Timing:**
- When time becomes critical
- Before deadline
- When competitors are moving faster

**Messaging:**
- "Deploy in days, not months."
- "Ship faster than your competitors."
- "From prototype to production in hours."

**Target Audience:**
- Solo founders with tight deadlines
- Startups in competitive markets
- Developers who need to ship fast

**Frequency:** Common (40%+ of users experience this)

---

### Switch Moment 4: Need Compliance (Education)

**Trigger:**
- Need FERPA/COPPA compliance
- Don't know how to implement compliance
- Realize compliance is required
- Can't serve education market without it

**Emotion:**
- Blocked (can't serve market without compliance)
- Uncertain (don't know how to implement)
- Frustrated (compliance is complex)
- Urgent (need compliance to serve market)

**Action:**
- Search for compliant solutions ("FERPA compliant AI platform", "education AI agent platform")
- Look for compliance features
- Discover Agent Factory (FERPA/COPPA compliant)
- Sign up for education tier

**Timing:**
- When targeting education market
- Before serving education customers
- When compliance becomes requirement

**Messaging:**
- "FERPA/COPPA compliant out of the box."
- "Serve education market without compliance headaches."
- "Education-ready infrastructure."

**Target Audience:**
- Education startups
- EdTech companies
- Institutions deploying AI tools

**Frequency:** Less common (10%+ of users, but high-value)

---

### Switch Moment 5: Hit Limitations of Current Solution

**Trigger:**
- Current solution (no-code, framework) hits limitations
- Need more control/flexibility
- Want code-first approach
- Outgrow current solution

**Emotion:**
- Frustrated (hit limitations)
- Constrained (can't do what you need)
- Limited (solution doesn't fit)
- Ready to upgrade (need better solution)

**Action:**
- Search for more flexible solutions ("code-first AI platform", "developer-friendly AI platform")
- Look for code-first alternatives
- Discover Agent Factory (code-first, full control)
- Switch to Agent Factory

**Timing:**
- After hitting limitations
- When need more control
- When ready to upgrade

**Messaging:**
- "Code-first, full control."
- "Built by developers, for developers."
- "No limitations, full flexibility."

**Target Audience:**
- Developers who've outgrown no-code
- Teams who need more control
- Users who want code-first approach

**Frequency:** Moderate (30%+ of users experience this)
