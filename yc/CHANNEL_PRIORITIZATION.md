# Channel Prioritization - Agent Factory

**For:** Disciplined Entrepreneurship, GTM Strategy, Distribution  
**Last Updated:** 2024-01-XX

---

## Overview

This document prioritizes distribution channels based on reach, conversion, cost, and speed. Focusing on the best channels first maximizes growth and ROI.

---

## Channel Evaluation Framework

### Evaluation Criteria
1. **Reach:** How many potential customers can we reach?
2. **Conversion:** What % will convert to customers?
3. **Cost:** What's the cost per acquisition (CAC)?
4. **Speed:** How fast can we execute?
5. **Scalability:** Can we scale this channel?

### Scoring
- **High:** 3 points
- **Medium:** 2 points
- **Low:** 1 point

## Channel Analysis

### Channel 1: Open Source → GitHub → Developer Communities

**Description:** Open-source core platform, GitHub as distribution channel, developer communities (Hacker News, Reddit) for awareness

**Reach:** ✅ **High** (millions of developers on GitHub, Hacker News, Reddit)
- GitHub: 100M+ developers
- Hacker News: 5M+ monthly visitors
- Reddit: 50M+ monthly visitors in relevant subreddits

**Conversion:** ⚠️ **Medium** (5-10% signup rate typical)
- GitHub stars → signups: 2-5%
- Hacker News → signups: 5-10%
- Reddit → signups: 3-7%

**Cost:** ✅ **Low** ($0-100/month)
- Open source: $0
- GitHub: $0
- Hacker News/Reddit: $0 (organic)
- Content creation: $0-100/month (time cost)

**Speed:** ✅ **High** (can execute immediately)
- Open source: Already done
- GitHub: Can launch today
- Content: Can publish immediately

**Scalability:** ✅ **High** (network effects, organic growth)
- GitHub stars create social proof
- Content creates SEO value
- Community creates network effects

**Total Score:** 14/15 (High priority)

**Pros:**
- Low cost, high reach
- Developer-focused (matches target audience)
- Network effects (GitHub stars, community)
- SEO value (content)

**Cons:**
- Conversion may be lower than paid channels
- Requires content creation
- Competitive (many open-source projects)

**Execution Plan:**
1. Launch open-source core (Q1 2024)
2. Publish on GitHub (Q1 2024)
3. Share on Hacker News, Reddit (Q1 2024)
4. Create content (blog posts, tutorials) (Q1-Q2 2024)
5. Build community (Discord, GitHub Discussions) (Q2 2024)

**Target Metrics:**
- GitHub Stars: 500 (3 months), 1,000 (6 months), 5,000 (12 months)
- Signups from GitHub: 50 (3 months), 200 (6 months), 1,000 (12 months)
- CAC: <$10

### Channel 2: Content Marketing → SEO

**Description:** Blog posts, tutorials, case studies optimized for SEO to drive organic traffic

**Reach:** ✅ **High** (millions of searches for relevant keywords)
- "AI agent builder": 1,000+ monthly searches
- "build AI agent": 5,000+ monthly searches
- "AI agent platform": 500+ monthly searches
- Long-tail keywords: 10,000+ monthly searches

**Conversion:** ⚠️ **Medium** (3-7% signup rate typical)
- SEO traffic → signups: 3-7%
- Depends on keyword intent and landing page quality

**Cost:** ✅ **Low** ($0-500/month)
- Content creation: $0-500/month (time or freelance)
- SEO tools: $0-100/month (free tiers available)
- Hosting: $0 (use existing infrastructure)

**Speed:** ⚠️ **Medium** (takes 3-6 months to see results)
- Content creation: 1-2 weeks per post
- SEO ranking: 3-6 months to rank
- Immediate traffic: Low (need to build authority)

**Scalability:** ✅ **High** (content compounds over time)
- Content creates long-term SEO value
- More content = more traffic
- Network effects (backlinks, shares)

**Total Score:** 12/15 (High priority)

**Pros:**
- Long-term value (content compounds)
- Low cost
- High reach (SEO traffic)
- Builds brand authority

**Cons:**
- Slow to see results (3-6 months)
- Requires content creation
- Competitive (need to rank)

**Execution Plan:**
1. Create content calendar (Q1 2024)
2. Publish 4 blog posts (Q1 2024)
3. Optimize for SEO (ongoing)
4. Build backlinks (Q2-Q4 2024)
5. Scale content (12+ posts/year)

**Target Metrics:**
- Monthly Organic Traffic: 1K (3 months), 5K (6 months), 20K (12 months)
- Signups from SEO: 30 (3 months), 250 (6 months), 1,000 (12 months)
- CAC: <$20

### Channel 3: Education Partnership → McGraw Hill

**Description:** Strategic partnership with McGraw Hill Education for education market distribution

**Reach:** ⚠️ **Medium** (thousands of education institutions)
- McGraw Hill: 2,000+ higher ed institutions
- Education market: 5,000+ institutions total
- Limited to education market (not general developers)

**Conversion:** ✅ **High** (20-30% conversion typical for partnerships)
- Partnership referrals: 20-30% conversion
- High trust (McGraw Hill endorsement)
- Pre-qualified leads

**Cost:** ✅ **Low** ($0-1K/month)
- Partnership development: $0 (already established)
- Co-marketing: $0-1K/month (events, materials)
- Revenue share: 10-20% (if applicable)

**Speed:** ⚠️ **Medium** (6-12 months to see results)
- Partnership activation: 1-2 months
- Sales cycles: 6-12 months (education)
- First customers: 3-6 months

**Scalability:** ⚠️ **Medium** (limited by partnership scope)
- Limited to education market
- Depends on partnership success
- Can expand to other education partners

**Total Score:** 11/15 (Medium-high priority)

**Pros:**
- High conversion (partnership referrals)
- Low cost (partnership already exists)
- Defensible (education market barriers)
- High-value customers ($20K+/year)

**Cons:**
- Limited reach (education only)
- Slow sales cycles (6-12 months)
- Depends on partnership success

**Execution Plan:**
1. Activate McGraw Hill partnership (Q1 2024)
2. Create co-marketing materials (Q1 2024)
3. Identify pilot institutions (Q1-Q2 2024)
4. Run pilot programs (Q2-Q3 2024)
5. Scale partnership (Q3-Q4 2024)

**Target Metrics:**
- Education Customers: 2 (6 months), 5 (12 months), 15 (24 months)
- Education Revenue: $40K (6 months), $100K (12 months), $750K (24 months)
- CAC: <$5K (partnership cost amortized)

### Channel 4: Referral/Invite System

**Description:** In-product referral system where users refer friends

**Reach:** ⚠️ **Medium** (depends on user base)
- Limited by current user base
- Grows with user base (network effects)
- Viral coefficient determines reach

**Conversion:** ✅ **High** (30-50% conversion typical for referrals)
- Referral signups: 30-50% conversion
- High trust (friend referral)
- Pre-qualified (users refer similar users)

**Cost:** ✅ **Low** ($0-500/month)
- Development: $0 (telemetry exists, needs UI)
- Rewards: $0-500/month (free credits, features)
- Infrastructure: $0 (use existing)

**Speed:** ✅ **High** (can implement quickly)
- Development: 1-2 weeks
- Launch: Immediate
- Results: 1-2 months to see impact

**Scalability:** ✅ **High** (network effects, viral growth)
- Each user brings new users
- Viral coefficient determines growth
- Self-reinforcing (more users → more referrals)

**Total Score:** 13/15 (High priority)

**Pros:**
- High conversion (referral trust)
- Low cost
- Fast to implement
- Network effects (viral growth)

**Cons:**
- Limited by user base (need users first)
- Requires activation (users must activate first)
- Rewards cost (free credits, features)

**Execution Plan:**
1. Design referral system (Q1 2024)
2. Implement referral UI (Q1 2024)
3. Launch referral system (Q1 2024)
4. Optimize referral flow (Q2 2024)
5. Scale rewards (Q2-Q4 2024)

**Target Metrics:**
- Referral Rate: >10% (3 months), >20% (6 months), >30% (12 months)
- Viral Coefficient: >0.5 (3 months), >1.0 (6 months), >1.5 (12 months)
- Referrals Converted: 10 (3 months), 100 (6 months), 600 (12 months)
- CAC: <$5 (reward cost)

### Channel 5: Paid Ads (Google, LinkedIn)

**Description:** Paid advertising on Google Ads and LinkedIn Ads

**Reach:** ✅ **High** (millions of impressions possible)
- Google: Millions of searches for relevant keywords
- LinkedIn: Millions of professionals in target segments

**Conversion:** ⚠️ **Medium** (2-5% signup rate typical)
- Google Ads: 2-5% signup rate
- LinkedIn Ads: 1-3% signup rate
- Depends on targeting and ad quality

**Cost:** ❌ **High** ($500-2K/month)
- Google Ads: $500-1K/month (CPC $2-5)
- LinkedIn Ads: $500-1K/month (CPC $5-10)
- Creative: $0-500/month (time or freelance)

**Speed:** ✅ **High** (can launch immediately)
- Setup: 1-2 days
- Launch: Immediate
- Results: Immediate (but need optimization)

**Scalability:** ⚠️ **Medium** (limited by budget)
- Limited by ad budget
- CAC increases with scale
- Need to optimize continuously

**Total Score:** 10/15 (Medium priority)

**Pros:**
- High reach
- Fast to launch
- Immediate results
- Scalable with budget

**Cons:**
- High cost (CAC $50-200)
- Requires optimization
- Competitive (many advertisers)
- Limited scalability (budget constraint)

**Execution Plan:**
1. Set up Google Ads account (Q2 2024)
2. Create ad campaigns (Q2 2024)
3. Test and optimize (Q2-Q4 2024)
4. Scale if profitable (Q3-Q4 2024)

**Target Metrics:**
- Monthly Ad Spend: $500 (3 months), $1K (6 months), $2K (12 months)
- Signups from Ads: 10 (3 months), 50 (6 months), 200 (12 months)
- CAC: <$50 (target), <$100 (acceptable)
