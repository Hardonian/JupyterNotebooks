## Assumptions & Rationale

### User Growth Assumptions
- **3 Months:** Launch + initial marketing → 100 signups
- **6 Months:** Content marketing + referrals → 500 signups
- **12 Months:** Marketplace flywheel + SEO → 2,000 signups

### Revenue Assumptions
- **Conversion Rate:** 10% free → paid (conservative)
- **Average Revenue per User:** $100/month (mix of tiers)
- **Churn Rate:** 5%/month (improving over time)

### Growth Assumptions
- **CAC:** Decreases as content marketing and referrals scale
- **Viral Coefficient:** Increases as marketplace flywheel matures
- **Activation Rate:** Improves as onboarding optimizes

## Risk Factors

### Downside Risks
- **User Growth:** Slower than expected (mitigation: multiple channels)
- **Conversion:** Lower than 10% (mitigation: pricing tests, optimization)
- **Churn:** Higher than 5% (mitigation: activation optimization, retention)

### Upside Opportunities
- **Marketplace Flywheel:** Could accelerate growth beyond targets
- **Enterprise:** Could drive higher ARPU than expected
- **Partnerships:** Could provide distribution boost
