**For:** Techstars, Disciplined Entrepreneurship, Market Development

## Overview

This document outlines our engagement strategy with the education ecosystem to establish Agent Factory as the platform for AI agents in education. Our beachhead market is education, and strategic ecosystem engagement is critical for success.

---

## Education Market Overview

### Market Size
- **Higher Education:** 4,000+ institutions in US, 20,000+ globally
- **K-12:** 130,000+ schools in US
- **Corporate Training:** $366B global market
- **Total Addressable Market:** $1.5B+ for education AI tools

### Key Characteristics
- **High Barriers:** FERPA/COPPA compliance required
- **Long Sales Cycles:** 6-12 months typical
- **Budget Cycles:** Annual (July-September)
- **Decision Makers:** IT, Academic Affairs, Provost Office
