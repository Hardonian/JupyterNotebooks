### [Company Name] - Customer Support Automation

**Company:** [Company Name]  
**Industry:** E-commerce  
**Size:** 50 employees, $5M ARR  
**Location:** San Francisco, CA  
**Plan:** Business ($499/month)  
**Timeline:** Started January 2024

---

## The Challenge

**Problem Statement:**
Company was receiving 500+ customer support requests per week. Support team was overwhelmed, response times were slow, and costs were high.

**Specific Challenges:**
1. High support volume (500+ requests/week)
2. Slow response times (24-48 hours)
3. High support costs ($50K+/year)
4. Inconsistent quality

**Previous Solutions:**
- Tried building custom bot (6 months, $100K, failed)
- Tried outsourcing (expensive, low quality)
- Tried hiring more support staff (expensive, hard to scale)

**Impact of Problem:**
- Customer satisfaction: 6/10
- Support costs: $50K+/year
- Lost sales due to slow support

---

## The Solution

**Why Agent Factory:**
Needed production-ready solution quickly. Agent Factory provided billing, multi-tenancy, and compliance out of the box.

**Implementation:**
- Week 1: Set up Agent Factory, created support bot agent
- Week 2: Trained bot on support docs, tested
- Week 3: Deployed to production
- Total time: 3 weeks

**What They Built:**
- Customer support bot agent
- Integrated with their help desk
- Handles 70% of support requests automatically

---

## The Results

**Quantitative Results:**
- Time saved: 20 hours/week per support agent
- Cost savings: $30K/year (reduced support staff)
- ROI: 600% (first year)
- Agents deployed: 1
- Support requests handled: 350/week (70% automation)
- Response time: < 1 minute (vs. 24-48 hours)

**Qualitative Results:**
- Customer satisfaction: 8.5/10 (up from 6/10)
- Support team happier (less repetitive work)
- Faster resolution times
- 24/7 support availability

**Before vs. After:**
- Before: 5 support agents, 24-48 hour response time, $50K/year cost
- After: 2 support agents, < 1 minute response time, $20K/year cost (including Agent Factory)
