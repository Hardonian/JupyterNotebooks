- Education focus (FERPA, LMS) is our moat.
- Switching costs: users build on our platform, hard to leave.

#### Question 2: "What if OpenAI/Anthropic changes pricing?"

**Answer:**
- We support multiple LLM providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, can add more).

- Usage-based pricing passes costs to customers.
- Cost optimization (caching, batching) reduces impact.
- Can consider self-hosting models for enterprise.

**Evidence:**
- Multiple provider support (`agent_factory/integrations/`)
- Cost tracking (`agent_factory/financial/cost_tracker.py`)
- Usage-based pricing (billing plans)
