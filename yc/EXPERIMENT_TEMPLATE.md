**ID:** EXP-001  
**Date Created:** 2024-01-15  
**Owner:** Product Team

### Hypothesis

**Hypothesis Statement:**
"Developers searching for 'AI agent builder' will sign up for Agent Factory"

**Hypothesis Type:**
- [x] Growth Hypothesis

**Related Hypothesis:** `/yc/HYPOTHESES.md#growth-hypothesis-content-marketing`

### Experiment Design

**What We're Testing:**
- Problem awareness (do developers search for this?)
- Signup intent (will they sign up?)
- Messaging effectiveness (does our messaging resonate?)

**Experiment Description:**
Create SEO-optimized landing page for "AI agent builder" keyword. Publish blog post and share on Hacker News, Reddit, Twitter. Measure organic traffic and signup rate.

**Method:**
- [x] Landing Page Test
- [x] Usage Metrics

### Success Criteria

**Success Criteria:**
- >100 visitors from organic search
- >5% signup rate (5+ signups)

**Minimum Success:**
- >50 visitors, >3% signup rate (2+ signups)

**Target Success:**
- >500 visitors, >5% signup rate (25+ signups)

**Stretch Goal:**
- >1000 visitors, >10% signup rate (100+ signups)

**Failure Criteria:**
- <50 visitors after 1 week
- <1% signup rate
- No signups from organic search

### Metrics

**Primary Metrics:**
1. Organic Traffic: >500 visitors
2. Signup Rate: >5%
3. Signups: >25

**Secondary Metrics:**
1. Time on Page: >2 minutes
2. Bounce Rate: <60%

**How We'll Measure:**
Google Analytics for traffic, Mixpanel for signups, UTM tracking for attribution
