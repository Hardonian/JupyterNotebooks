# Partner Program

**Last Updated:** 2024-01-XX  
**Purpose:** Comprehensive partner program for Agent Factory Platform

---

## Partner Program Overview

**Mission:** Build a thriving ecosystem of partners to extend Agent Factory's reach and capabilities  
**Types:** Technology Partners, Integration Partners, Reseller Partners, Education Partners  
**Benefits:** Revenue sharing, co-marketing, technical support, priority access

---

## Partner Types

### Technology Partners

**Definition:** Companies that integrate with Agent Factory  
**Examples:** LMS providers, cloud providers, developer tools  
**Benefits:**
- Technical integration support
- Co-marketing opportunities
- Joint go-to-market
- Revenue sharing

**Requirements:**
- Technical integration
- Joint marketing commitment
- Support alignment
- Contract agreement

---

### Integration Partners

**Definition:** Companies that build integrations for Agent Factory  
**Examples:** Third-party tools, services, platforms  
**Benefits:**
- Marketplace listing
- Technical support
- Co-marketing
- Revenue sharing

**Requirements:**
- Working integration
- Documentation
- Support commitment
- Quality standards

---

### Reseller Partners

**Definition:** Companies that resell Agent Factory  
**Examples:** Agencies, consultants, system integrators  
**Benefits:**
- Discounted pricing
- Sales support
- Co-marketing
- Revenue sharing

**Requirements:**
- Sales commitment
- Customer support
- Training completion
- Contract agreement

---

### Education Partners

**Definition:** Educational institutions and organizations  
**Examples:** Universities, K-12 districts, training organizations  
**Benefits:**
- Education-specific features
- Co-marketing
- Joint research
- Community building

**Requirements:**
- Education focus
- Student data compliance
- Partnership commitment
- Contract agreement
