# Metrics Framework

**Last Updated:** 2024-01-XX  
**Purpose:** Comprehensive metrics framework for Agent Factory Platform

---

## Metrics Overview

**Purpose:** Measure business performance, product usage, customer success, and operational health  
**Categories:** Business, Product, Customer, Operations  
**Frequency:** Real-time, daily, weekly, monthly

---

## Business Metrics

### Revenue Metrics

**Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR):**
- Definition: Recurring revenue per month
- Target: $100K+ by Year 1
- Frequency: Monthly

**Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR):**
- Definition: MRR × 12
- Target: $1.2M+ by Year 1
- Frequency: Monthly

**Revenue Growth Rate:**
- Definition: Month-over-month growth
- Target: 10%+ monthly
- Frequency: Monthly

---

### Customer Metrics

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):**
- Definition: Total acquisition cost / new customers
- Target: <$500
- Frequency: Monthly

**Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):**
- Definition: Average revenue per customer × average lifetime
- Target: >$5,000
- Frequency: Monthly

**LTV:CAC Ratio:**
- Definition: LTV / CAC
- Target: >3:1
- Frequency: Monthly

---

### Growth Metrics

**New Customers:**
- Definition: Customers acquired per period
- Target: 100+ by Year 1
- Frequency: Weekly

**Customer Retention Rate:**
- Definition: Customers retained / total customers
- Target: 90%+
- Frequency: Monthly

**Net Revenue Retention:**
- Definition: (Starting MRR + Expansion - Churn) / Starting MRR
- Target: 100%+
- Frequency: Monthly

---

## Product Metrics

### Usage Metrics

**Active Agents:**
- Definition: Agents with activity in period
- Target: 1,000+ by Year 1
- Frequency: Daily

**API Calls:**
- Definition: Total API calls per period
- Target: 10M+ per month by Year 1
- Frequency: Daily

**Feature Adoption:**
- Definition: % of users using feature
- Target: 50%+ for key features
- Frequency: Weekly

---

### Engagement Metrics

**Daily Active Users (DAU):**
- Definition: Users active per day
- Target: 500+ by Year 1
- Frequency: Daily

**Monthly Active Users (MAU):**
- Definition: Users active per month
- Target: 1,000+ by Year 1
- Frequency: Monthly

**Session Duration:**
- Definition: Average session length
- Target: 15+ minutes
- Frequency: Daily

---

### Quality Metrics

**Uptime:**
- Definition: % of time service available
- Target: 99.9%+
- Frequency: Real-time

**Error Rate:**
- Definition: % of requests with errors
- Target: <0.1%
- Frequency: Real-time

**Response Time:**
- Definition: Average API response time
- Target: <200ms (p95)
- Frequency: Real-time

---

## Customer Metrics

### Satisfaction Metrics

**Net Promoter Score (NPS):**
- Definition: % promoters - % detractors
- Target: 50+
- Frequency: Quarterly

**Customer Satisfaction (CSAT):**
- Definition: Average satisfaction score
- Target: 4.5/5
- Frequency: After interactions

**Customer Effort Score (CES):**
- Definition: Ease of use score
- Target: <2 (1-5 scale)
- Frequency: After interactions

---

### Success Metrics

**Time to First Value:**
- Definition: Time to first agent deployment
- Target: <7 days
- Frequency: Weekly

**Health Score:**
- Definition: Composite health score (0-100)
- Target: 80+ average
- Frequency: Weekly

**Expansion Rate:**
- Definition: % of customers with expansion
- Target: 20%+
- Frequency: Monthly

---
