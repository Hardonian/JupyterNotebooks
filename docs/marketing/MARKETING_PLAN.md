# Marketing Plan

**Founder, CEO & Operator:** Scott Hardie  
**Last Updated:** 2024-01-XX  
**Purpose:** Comprehensive 12-month marketing plan for Agent Factory Platform

**Note from Founder:** After 15+ years in EdTech sales and solution architecture, I've learned that authentic storytelling and education-focused content drive results. This plan leverages my network in education technology and business development expertise, and understanding of developer communities.

---

## Executive Summary

**Marketing Objective:** Generate 1,000+ qualified leads and 100+ customers in Year 1  
**Marketing Budget:** $150K-$200K  
**Primary Channels:** Content marketing, SEO, paid advertising, partnerships, events  
**Target Segments:** Developers, educational institutions, enterprise teams

## Target Audience

### Primary Personas

**1. Solo Founders / Small Teams**
- **Demographics:** 25-45, technical background
- **Pain Points:** Limited resources, need to ship fast
- **Channels:** Developer communities, content marketing
- **Messaging:** "Ship in days, not months"

**2. Educational Institutions**
- **Demographics:** IT directors, administrators, faculty
- **Pain Points:** Lack of AI expertise, compliance requirements
- **Channels:** Education conferences, partnerships
- **Messaging:** "Education-focused, FERPA compliant"

**3. Enterprise Teams**
- **Demographics:** CTOs, engineering leads, product managers
- **Pain Points:** Building infrastructure, scaling challenges
- **Channels:** Enterprise events, sales outreach
- **Messaging:** "Production-ready, scalable, supported"
