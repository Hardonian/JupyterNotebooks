## Automation Workflows

### Workflow 1: Lead Nurturing

**Trigger:** New lead captured

**Steps:**

**1. Immediate (0-1 hour):**
- Send welcome email
- Add to CRM
- Assign lead score
- Route to sales (if qualified)

**2. Day 1:**
- Send product overview
- Share case studies
- Provide resources
- Track engagement

**3. Day 3:**
- Send educational content
- Invite to webinar
- Share success stories
- Monitor engagement

**4. Day 7:**
- Send demo invitation
- Provide pricing information
- Share ROI calculator
- Check qualification status

**5. Day 14:**
- Send special offer
- Schedule sales call
- Provide trial access
- Final qualification check

---

### Workflow 2: Event Marketing

**Trigger:** Event registration

**Steps:**

**1. Pre-Event (Automated):**
- Send confirmation email
- Add to calendar
- Send reminder emails
- Share event resources

**2. During Event (Automated):**
- Send live updates
- Share resources
- Collect feedback
- Monitor engagement

**3. Post-Event (Automated):**
- Send thank you email
- Share recording/resources
- Request feedback
- Nurture leads

---

### Workflow 3: Content Promotion

**Trigger:** New content published

**Steps:**

**1. Immediate (Automated):**
- Publish to blog
- Share on social media
- Send email newsletter
- Update website

**2. Day 1 (Automated):**
- Social media reposts
- Community sharing
- Influencer outreach
- Paid promotion

**3. Week 1 (Automated):**
- Email follow-up
- Additional promotion
- Performance analysis
- Optimization

## Metrics & KPIs

### Lead Generation Metrics

- **Lead Volume:** Number of leads generated
- **Lead Quality:** Qualified lead percentage
- **Cost per Lead:** Marketing spend / leads
- **Conversion Rate:** Leads to customers

---

### Email Marketing Metrics

- **Open Rate:** % of emails opened
- **Click-Through Rate:** % of clicks
- **Conversion Rate:** % of conversions
- **Unsubscribe Rate:** % of unsubscribes

---

### Content Marketing Metrics

- **Traffic:** Website visitors
- **Engagement:** Time on page, bounce rate
- **Shares:** Social media shares
- **Conversions:** Content to lead conversions

---

### ROI Metrics

- **Marketing ROI:** Revenue / Marketing spend
- **Customer Acquisition Cost:** Marketing spend / customers
- **Lifetime Value:** Customer LTV
- **Payback Period:** Time to recover CAC
