## Customer Health Score

### Health Score Components

**1. Product Usage (40%)**
- Active agents
- API calls
- Feature adoption
- Login frequency

**2. Engagement (30%)**
- Support tickets
- Training attendance
- Community participation
- Feedback provided

**3. Business Outcomes (20%)**
- Goals achieved
- ROI realized
- Value demonstrated
- Success metrics met

**4. Relationship (10%)**
- NPS score
- Referenceability
- Advocacy
- Satisfaction

---

### Health Score Calculation

**Formula:**
```
Health Score = (Usage × 0.4) + (Engagement × 0.3) + 
               (Outcomes × 0.2) + (Relationship × 0.1)
```

**Scoring:**
- **Healthy (80-100):** Green
- **At Risk (60-79):** Yellow
- **Critical (<60):** Red

### Churn Prevention Playbook

**Early Warning Signs:**
- Decreased usage
- Support tickets increase
- Negative feedback
- No engagement

**Intervention Steps:**
1. Identify at-risk customer
2. Reach out immediately
3. Understand issues
4. Provide solutions
5. Re-engage
6. Monitor progress

**Escalation:**
- CSM intervention
- Executive involvement
- Custom solutions
- Retention offers
