## Contract Best Practices

### Before Signing

**1. Review Thoroughly**
- Read entire contract
- Understand all terms
- Ask questions
- Get legal review

**2. Negotiate Terms**
- Price and payment terms
- Service levels
- Termination rights
- Liability limits

**3. Document Everything**
- All communications
- Agreed changes
- Verbal commitments
- Email confirmations

---

### After Signing

**1. Store Securely**
- Keep signed copies
- Store in secure location
- Backup copies
- Share with team

**2. Monitor Compliance**
- Track obligations
- Monitor performance
- Document issues
- Maintain records

**3. Review Regularly**
- Annual review
- Update as needed
- Renegotiate if necessary
- Terminate if appropriate
