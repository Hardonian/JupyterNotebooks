# Sales Qualification Framework

**Last Updated:** 2024-01-XX  
**Purpose:** Standardized qualification framework for sales opportunities

---

## Overview

This framework helps sales reps qualify opportunities using BANT and MEDDIC methodologies.

**Primary Framework:** BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)  
**Advanced Framework:** MEDDIC (for enterprise deals)

## Qualification Scripts

### Initial Qualification Call

**Opening (2 min):**
"Thanks for your interest in Agent Factory. I'd like to understand your needs better. Is now a good time?"

**Budget (3 min):**
"Do you have budget allocated for this type of solution? What's your budget range?"

**Authority (3 min):**
"Who would be involved in making this decision? Are you the decision maker?"

**Need (5 min):**
"What problem are you trying to solve? Why is this important now?"

**Timeline (3 min):**
"When do you need this solved? What's driving the timeline?"

**Closing (2 min):**
"Based on what you've shared, I think Agent Factory could be a good fit. Would you like to see a demo?"

## Disqualification Criteria

### Disqualify If:

1. **No Budget**
   - No budget allocated
   - No budget approval process
   - Budget too small

2. **No Authority**
   - Not decision maker
   - Can't get decision maker involved
   - Unclear decision process

3. **No Need**
   - Vague requirements
   - No urgency
   - Just exploring

4. **No Timeline**
   - No deadline
   - Timeline too far out (>12 months)
   - No driving factors
