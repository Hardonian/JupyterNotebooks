## Overview

This document defines the sales process, stages, and procedures for selling Agent Factory Platform.

**Sales Model:** Hybrid (self-service + sales-assisted)  
**Primary Channels:** Self-service, Inside sales, Enterprise sales  
**Average Sales Cycle:** 2-4 weeks (self-service), 2-6 months (enterprise)

---

## Sales Stages

### Stage 1: Lead Generation

**Objective:** Generate qualified leads

**Activities:**
- Content marketing
- SEO and organic search
- Paid advertising
- Partnerships
- Referrals
- Events and webinars

**Qualification Criteria:**
- Need for AI agents
- Budget available
- Decision-making authority
- Timeline defined

**Output:** Qualified lead

---

### Stage 2: Discovery

**Objective:** Understand customer needs and pain points

**Activities:**
- Initial conversation
- Needs assessment
- Pain point identification
- Use case exploration
- Budget discussion
- Timeline discussion

**Discovery Questions:**
- What are you trying to accomplish?
- What challenges are you facing?
- What have you tried before?
- What's your timeline?
- What's your budget?
- Who's involved in the decision?

**Output:** Qualified opportunity

**Duration:** 1-2 calls (30-60 minutes each)

---

### Stage 3: Qualification

**Objective:** Confirm fit and prioritize opportunities

**Qualification Framework:** BANT
- **Budget:** Do they have budget?
- **Authority:** Can they make the decision?
- **Need:** Do they have a clear need?
- **Timeline:** When do they need to solve this?

**Alternative Framework:** MEDDIC
- **Metrics:** What metrics matter?
- **Economic Buyer:** Who makes the decision?
- **Decision Criteria:** What are their criteria?
- **Decision Process:** How do they decide?
- **Identify Pain:** What's the pain?
- **Champion:** Who's our internal advocate?

**Output:** Qualified opportunity with BANT/MEDDIC

**Duration:** 1 call (30 minutes)

---

### Stage 4: Demo

**Objective:** Show value and address concerns

**Activities:**
- Product demonstration
- Use case walkthrough
- Q&A session
- Objection handling
- Next steps discussion

**Demo Script:**
- Introduction (5 min)
- Problem statement (5 min)
- Solution overview (10 min)
- Live demo (20 min)
- Use case examples (10 min)
- Pricing discussion (5 min)
- Q&A (10 min)
- Next steps (5 min)

**Output:** Demo completed, interest confirmed

**Duration:** 1 call (60-90 minutes)

---

### Stage 5: Proposal

**Objective:** Present formal proposal

**Activities:**
- Proposal creation
- Pricing discussion
- Terms negotiation
- Proposal presentation
- Follow-up

**Proposal Components:**
- Executive summary
- Solution overview
- Pricing and terms
- Implementation timeline
- Success metrics
- Next steps

**Output:** Proposal sent

**Duration:** 3-5 days

---

### Stage 6: Negotiation

**Objective:** Finalize terms and pricing

**Activities:**
- Terms discussion
- Pricing negotiation
- Contract review
- Legal review
- Approval process

**Negotiation Guidelines:**
- Know your walk-away point
- Understand their constraints
- Find win-win solutions
- Document everything
- Get approvals

**Output:** Terms agreed

**Duration:** 1-2 weeks

---

### Stage 7: Closing

**Objective:** Sign contract and begin onboarding

**Activities:**
- Contract execution
- Payment processing
- Account setup
- Onboarding kickoff
- Handoff to customer success

**Output:** Closed deal

**Duration:** 1-2 weeks

---

4. Demo

8. Onboarding

**Touchpoints:** 10-20 (multiple calls, meetings, demos)

## Sales Best Practices

### Discovery

1. **Ask Open Questions**
   - "Tell me about your current process..."
   - "What challenges are you facing?"
   - "What would success look like?"

2. **Listen Actively**
   - Don't interrupt
   - Take notes
   - Clarify understanding

3. **Identify Pain Points**
   - What's not working?
   - What's the cost of inaction?
   - What's the impact?

---

### Demo

1. **Customize for Audience**
   - Focus on their use case
   - Address their pain points
   - Show relevant features

2. **Tell a Story**
   - Problem → Solution → Results
   - Use real examples
   - Show before/after

3. **Handle Objections**
   - Acknowledge concerns
   - Provide evidence
   - Offer alternatives

---

### Closing

1. **Create Urgency**
   - Limited-time offers
   - Upcoming deadlines
   - Opportunity cost

2. **Address Concerns**
   - Be transparent
   - Provide guarantees
   - Offer trials

3. **Make It Easy**
   - Simple process
   - Clear next steps
   - Quick response
